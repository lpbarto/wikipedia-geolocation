Search




Find where an article is located


Search for a topic, article, person, food and even a concept.


We will find that Wikipedia article and we will pin it in the map.


Then if you want to continue reading it just click the pin, it will automatically route you to the Wikipedia article.










Discover




Search for cool stuff


Find a place that you like or a place that ispires you.


We will search for the most intresting things that originates there.


You can personalizehe range and the number of article that you want to show up.


Then if you want to continue reading one of them just click the pin, it will automatically route you to his Wikipedia article.










Now that you know how it works, choose a mode and give it a try.
